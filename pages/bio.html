Violeta López López is an interdisciplinary artist and musician working in the in-betweeness of fields with a multimedia approach that merges analog and digital, visuals, sound and words. Her work dives into the entanglement of materiality and virtuality, exploring connections too between language, literature and science. At the core of her work lies a poetical perspective from which she intends to free languages from their established use, looking for new ways of expressing the identity of the world and its perception.
